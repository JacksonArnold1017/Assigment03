A tribute to the lead singer of TOOL

Maynards Early Life

Maynard James Keenan (born James Herbert Keenan) was born in Ravenna, Ohio in 1964. At the age of 11 his mother, Judith Marie, suffered a cerebral aneurysm. She was left paralyzed from the waist down until her death.

In 1981, he joined the US army as a forward observer, but not too long after he left to study at West Point Prep School. Keenan sang in glee club, wrestled, and ran on the cross country team.
